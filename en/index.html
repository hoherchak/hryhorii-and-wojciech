How to get

How to get to Zakynthos

The closest airport is Zakynthos International Airport (ZTH), located approximately 7 minutes drive (4.2 km) from the reception venue.

We will update the information below later as tickets will become available.

From Warsaw From the rest of Poland From the US

Traveling from Warsaw

WizzAir has direct flights between Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) and Zakynthos each Tuesday, Saturday and Sunday.

Ryanair has direct flights between Warsaw Modlin Airport (WMI) and Zakynthos each Sunday.

Rainbow has direct charter flights between Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) and Zakynthos with outbound flights every Tuesday, Friday and Sunday and return flights on Tuesdays.

Stay longer

We encourage you to take this opportunity to stay longer in Zakynthos or explore Greece further.

  1. There are plenty offers from travel agencies that provide hotels and flights together from as low 1600 PLN per person for a week on the island. You can also find some options very close to the venue in Kalamaki staring from 2800 PLN (with two meals included per day).
    Already available on TUI website!!! Likely more offers would appear closer to the date.

  2. If you stay for longer vacation from Tuesday, September 8 to Sunday, September 13 – you can get return tickets starting from 920 PLN per person (including a small carry-on bag).
    Already available on WizzAir website for September 2026!!!

  3. If you stay for longer vacation from Sunday, September 6 to Sunday, September 13 - return tickets usually start from 750 PLN per person (including a small carry-on bag).
    These tickets would likely become available from December 14, 2025.

Stay for wedding only

If you'd prefer to stay only for the time of the wedding, we recommend flying to Zakynthos by Rainbow charter flight (~900 PLN one way with check-in baggage included) on Friday, September 11 and returning with WizzAir (~560 PLN one way without or ~860 PLN with baggage) on Sunday, September 13.
Tickets for Rainbow flights will likely be available from November 14, 2025. WizzAir flights for September 2026 already available.

You can search for more options on https://www.esky.pl/ or https://flights.booking.com/. There are plenty other affordable routes available, but they would usually require layover in Europe. Be cautious as some options require self-transfer (booking.com provides guarantees in such case)!

If you want to consider driving by car, it's 24 hours from Warsaw (1400 km).

Traveling from the rest of Poland

Stay longer

We encourage you to take this opportunity to stay longer in Zakynthos or explore Greece further. You can get offers even cheaper than round-trip tickets.

  1. There are plenty offers from travel agencies that provide hotels with breakfasts and flights from Katowice and Poznań airports together from as low 1600 PLN per person for a week on the island. You can also find some options very close to the venue in Kalamaki starting from 2800 PLN (with two meals included per day).
    Already available on TUI website!!! Likely more offers would appear closer to the date.

  2. Rainbow has direct charter flights from Kraków, Katowice, Poznań, Grańsk, Wrocław and Łódż. Round trip to Zakynthos would cost around 1800 PLN per person (check-in baggage included). They usually don't have return flights on Sunday, but it's a good option if you'd like to extend your trip for a bit longer.

Stay for wedding only

For cheaper options, we recommend considering getting to Warsaw by train or bus, and flying either from Chopin (WAW) or Modlin (WMI) airports, as WizzAir and Ryanair fly to Zakynthos only from there. See Warsaw options.

You can search for more options on https://www.esky.pl/ or https://flights.booking.com/. There are plenty affordable routes available, but they would usually require longer layover in Europe.

There are some options with layover that allows to go out to the city. Might be a great opportunity to visit Vienna or Zurich.

Be cautious as some options require self-transfer (booking.com provides guarantees in such case)!

Traveling from US

There are plenty flights from JFK and Newark airports starting from $550 per person (with carry-on bag included) and from San Francisco airport starting from $1000 per person (with carry-on bag included) operated by Swiss, Lufthansa or United. Check https://www.google.com/travel/flights for all options. There are so many options available, that surely you'll find something that suits you.

Taking into account a long flight, we recommend staying for a longer vacation. There are all-inclusive resorts on booking.com for that period on Zakynthos starting from $740 per person for a week available already. Likely more offers would appear closer to the date.
